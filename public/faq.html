Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our property management services.

Property Management

What services do you provide?

We offer comprehensive property management services including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, financial reporting, and 24/7 support. Our AI-driven approach ensures efficient and effective management of your property.

How do you handle maintenance requests?

We have a 24/7 maintenance hotline for emergencies. For non-emergency requests, tenants can submit tickets through our online portal. We coordinate with pre-approved contractors and seek owner approval for expenses above the agreed threshold.

What is your tenant screening process?

Our AI-powered screening process includes credit checks, background verification, employment verification, rental history review, and income validation. We ensure only qualified tenants are selected for your property.

Financial Questions

How is rent collected and distributed?

Rent is collected electronically through our secure online portal. Funds are typically distributed to owners by the 10th of each month, along with detailed financial statements and any maintenance receipts.

What fees do you charge?

Our management fee structure is transparent and based on a percentage of monthly rent. Additional services like professional photography or marketing for vacant properties may incur separate fees. Please visit our pricing page for detailed information.

How do you handle security deposits?

Security deposits are held in a separate trust account as required by law. Upon move-out, we conduct a thorough inspection and provide detailed documentation for any deductions before returning the remaining deposit.

Technology & Communication

How do you use AI in property management?

We leverage AI for market analysis, tenant screening, maintenance scheduling, and financial forecasting. This technology helps us make data-driven decisions and provide more efficient service.

How can I access information about my property?

Property owners have 24/7 access to our online portal where they can view real-time updates, financial reports, maintenance requests, and important documents.

What reports do you provide?

We provide monthly financial statements, maintenance reports, occupancy reports, and market analysis. All reports are available through our online portal and can be customized based on your needs.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is here to help you with any additional questions you may have.
