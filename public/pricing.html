Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the management fee?

Our management fee covers tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, financial reporting, and 24/7 support for both owners and tenants.

Are there any additional fees?

We maintain complete transparency. The only additional fees are for services like professional photography, marketing costs for vacant properties, and maintenance costs (with your approval).

Can I switch plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

What's the minimum contract length?

We offer flexible month-to-month agreements with no long-term commitments required.
